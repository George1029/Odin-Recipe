Breakfast Cereal

DESCRIPTION.

Hello in this instructable I will show you how to make that bowl of cereal you have long desired for in the morning!

Ingredients.

  • Spoon.
  • Bowl. I mean Like a really BIG bowl.
  • Milk(Almond Milk, Oat Milk, 2% low fat, idc.)
  • Your preferred cereal.

STEPS.

  1. Gather all supplies.
  2. Next, get your bowl and place it on the counter top or table. Then add cereal.
  3. Now it is time to add milk! Get milk, remove cap, pour onto cereal, then put cap back on milk.
  4. Finally, add your spoon And enjoy your bowl of cereal!
